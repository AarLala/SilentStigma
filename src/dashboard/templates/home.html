SilentStigma

SilentStigma is an observatory for mental health discourse in public comments. It groups related comments into themes without labeling individuals.

What you can explore

  • Comment and video metrics for mental health channels
  • A two dimensional map of discourse clusters
  • Keywords and patterns that summarize each cluster
  • CSV exports for your own quantitative analysis

Why this is useful

SilentStigma lets you see how people talk about mental health at scale without deciding in advance which topics matter. It surfaces patterns in public comments in a way that is transparent and ready for further research.

For individuals it offers a structured way to read how others describe their experiences which can make it easier to feel less alone and to find language for their own stories.

Designed for

  • Computational social scientists
  • Mental health communication researchers
  • Methodologists exploring unsupervised pipelines
